About

Why the name?

The Halting Problem is one of the fundamental unsolved problems in theoretical computer science. In basic terms, the problem analyzes how one could prove whether or not a program will eventually finish running, or is trapped in an infinite cycle somewhere. I don't pretend to know the halting solution, but I really like it as a moniker.


Why is this site so empty looking?

I avoid using fancy formatting on my pages, because it becomes less accessible, especially for people who use screenreaders when accessing the internet.
